# Product Case Writing

Use this skill to turn product experience into website-ready case content.

## Workflow

1. Read the relevant source material in `素材/`.
2. Extract: product positioning, user problem, role, key decisions, solution, metrics, and takeaway.
3. Draft concise Chinese content for the website.

## Suggested Structure

- 项目定位
- 背景与问题
- 我的角色
- 关键决策
- 核心方案
- 结果指标
- 方法沉淀

## Writing Rule

Show decisions and results, not just feature lists.
